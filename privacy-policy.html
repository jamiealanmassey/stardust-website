Privacy Policy

VR Privacy Policy

This section of our privacy policy concerns how we collect, use and process data from the video games we develop and distribute on various storefronts.

Data Collection Statement

Unless explicitly stated elsewhere in this policy we do not collect any unique or identifying information from our players for the purpose of tracking them or for selling to any third-party companies. We may collect data associated with SDKs that are used within our games that are hosted by third-party platforms for the purpose of delivering game services (including but not limited to: online leaderboards, achievements, cloud saving and so on), in these circumstances the data is held and processed by those third-party platforms.

If you wish to request access to your data or for your data to be removed on these platforms please contact them directly.

If you have any further queries or require assistance with the removal of data on those platforms please contact us directly via the e-mail address developer@stardustcollective.dev

Third-party privacy policies: [Steam] [Meta]

Information Usage

We may utilise aggregated datasets created from the aforementioned third-party platforms to analyse player behaviour or to ensure continued operations of the apps that we create; This information will not identify any one individual player.

Changes to Policy

The content of this policy may change at any time to fall within the limits of the law or to evolve with the needs of the games that we develop and create. By playing our games you agree to the terms and conditions of the Privacy Policy.
