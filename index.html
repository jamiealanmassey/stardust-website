Who are we?

A collective of passionate individuals that have joined forces to create unique and interesting experiences -- the team discovered each other during the 2020 GMTK GameJam where they developed the first version of Ziggy's Cosmic Adventures and after decided to take to the VR space!

Our Values

Fun
We want to create unique experiences that are memorable and a blast to play!
Quality
We believe the games that we create should aspire to a high standard of visuals and gameplay
Affordability
Although we aim for high quality games we want as many people to be able to play them as possible
